## Highlights

- Simplified a layout to improve scanability.
- Reused a single button style across three pages.
- Cut a page load animation from 1200ms to 500ms.

<Callout type="info">
Release early, release often. Small incremental changes compound into 
significant improvements over time.
</Callout>

## What I learned

Speed is a feature, but clarity is the multiplier. When the structure is clear,
polish becomes easier and faster. Focus on shipping incrementally rather than 
chasing perfection in one massive PR.
